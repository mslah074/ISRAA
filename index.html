Reading 📖 The Story
"The Hidden Talent"
Maya stood backstage, her hands shaking as she held her violin. She could hear the loud chatter of the audience through the heavy velvet curtains.

For weeks, Maya had practised her song until her fingers were sore, but now, her mind felt like a blank page.

Just then, she saw her younger brother, Leo, sitting in the front row, looking at the stage with wide, excited eyes. He was wearing the 'Good Luck' pin she had given him. Maya took a deep breath, straightened her shoulders, and stepped out into the bright lights. She didn't look at the crowd; she looked only at Leo and began to play.

Reading for Details 🔑 Answers

True or False? – Answers

False
Maya was playing the piano backstage.
→ She was holding her violin, not a piano.
True
Maya had been practising her song for several weeks.
→ "For weeks, Maya had practised her song…"
True
Leo was wearing a 'Good Luck' pin that Maya gave him.
→ "He was wearing the 'Good Luck' pin she had given him."
False
Maya looked at the whole crowd when she started playing.
→ She looked only at Leo, not the crowd.
False
Maya's mind felt blank because she had never practised.
→ She had practised a lot — she was just nervous.
